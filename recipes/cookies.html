Cookies

Description

These cookies will have you screaming with how delicious they are. Also because they have razors inside them.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Razors
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients.
  2. Bake at a fairly hot temperature, preferably in an oven.
  3. Slide as many razors as you possibly can inside each cookie.
  4. Enjoy knowing you're alive.
